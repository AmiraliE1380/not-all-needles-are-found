In twilight, artist Hippolyte Schinner falls and loses consciousness, awakening to Adelaide and her mother’s care. After recovering, he visits their modest apartment, bonding over a portrait session. Suspicious of Madame de Rouville's finances after losing his purse, he remains drawn to Adelaide. A chance encounter complicates his feelings, but she discreetly replaces his purse, rekindling hope. The vice-admiral defends Madame de Rouville, shifting the atmosphere. The story culminates in Hippolyte’s heartfelt proposal to Adelaide, uniting their fates amid lingering doubts.
