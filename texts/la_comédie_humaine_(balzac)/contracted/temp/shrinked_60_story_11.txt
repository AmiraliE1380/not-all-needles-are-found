Doctor Monsieur Bianchon shares a chilling tale about La Grande Bretèche, a dilapidated house near Vendôme, linked to the late Comtesse de Merret. Local notary Monsieur Regnault reveals that her will forbids entry or repairs for fifty years. He recounts her reclusive life after her husband’s death and her intention to leave the estate to the hospital with strict conditions. Meanwhile, a woman discusses Count Férédia's mysterious disappearance, suspecting his involvement in Madame de Merret's death. The story concludes with reflections on the emotional turmoil surrounding La Grande Bretèche.
