_Mouche_ is a card game where players receive five cards, with one designated as trumps. Players can choose to play or abstain, with low stakes involved. Players can exchange cards from the remaining deck, and the dealer can swap the trump card for one in their hand. The most powerful card is Mistigris, the knave of clubs.

Despite its simplicity, _mouche_ captivates players, revealing human greed and strategic thinking. At the hotel du Guenic, players contribute twenty counters each, a significant amount for the group.
