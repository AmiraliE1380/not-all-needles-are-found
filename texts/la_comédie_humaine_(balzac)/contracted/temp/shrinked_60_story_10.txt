Mademoiselle des Touches’ salon is celebrated for its grace. One evening, notable figures, including de Marsay, discussed the evolution of women in France since the July Revolution. As the night unfolded, guests shared personal stories, with Mademoiselle des Touches ensuring everyone remained engaged.
