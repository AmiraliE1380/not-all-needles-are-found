In September 1835, Mademoiselle du Rouvre married Comte Adam Laginski. Despite initial perceptions of frivolity, Adam gained respect and social standing. Their financial manager, Captain Thaddeus Paz, developed unrequited feelings for Clementine, complicating relationships. Thaddeus's infatuation with circus performer Malaga distracted him until he chose to leave Paris, prompting Clementine to realize her feelings for him. Three years later, she remained intrigued by Thaddeus, reflecting the complexities of love and societal expectations.
