**The Purse**  
*By Honoré De Balzac*  

In twilight, painter Hippolyte Schinner falls from a ladder and is rescued by Adelaide and her mother, leaving him enamored with Adelaide's beauty. After recovering, he visits their modest home, where he offers to paint Madame Leseigneur's portrait, deepening their bond. As they share stories of hardship, Hippolyte grapples with his feelings for Adelaide amid social barriers, setting the stage for a complex relationship.

############# MIDDLE OF THE STORY. #############
################################################
In a fading monarchy, an aged Chevalier plays cards with Madame de Rouville and her daughter, Adelaide, who grows fond of him. Young painter Hippolyte falls for Adelaide but becomes suspicious of their financial situation after losing his purse. Despite heartbreak, he is drawn to her. A chance encounter reveals rumors about Madame de Rouville, complicating his feelings. When he visits again, Adelaide replaces his lost purse, rekindling hope. The arrival of a vice-admiral defends their honor, leading to Hippolyte's heartfelt proposal to Adelaide, culminating in unity and love amidst societal struggles.
