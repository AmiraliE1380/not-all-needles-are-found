The young man, dressed elegantly with white silk stockings and a cloak, appeared to have just come from a social event.

As he contemplated the house, three apprentices from the loft above playfully sprinkled him with shaving cream, but he remained indifferent. Suddenly, a young girl appeared at the window, her beauty reminiscent of classical art, but she quickly retreated, embarrassed by being seen. This fleeting moment left the young man disheartened.

Meanwhile, the shopkeeper, Monsieur Guillaume, opened his store, known for its extensive inventory and honest dealings. Despite the shop's unassuming appearance, Guillaume was a savvy businessman, adept at securing profits without the need for flattery or gifts, earning a reputation among local traders for his shrewdness.
